On the subject of chess and management, the lawyer with psychological background and chess grandmaster Michael Hoffmann offers the event "Chess Strategic Thinking for managers".

Grandmaster Hoffmann shows how the analytical procedure and the decision-making processes of a chess master run and how these skills and insights can profitably be used in your day-to-day business.

How can the problem solving skills, objectivity and flexibility of grandmastering thinking be used to avoid typical mistakes in thinking and to optimize strategic success? How can ratio and intuition be reconciled?
